Welcome to 2015 Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake

Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake is a spectacular air show that will entertain and ENTRALL you with VINTAGE AIRCRAFT, CUTTING EDGE TECHNOLOGY, DOG FIGHTING SIMULATIONS, PRECISION aerial acrobatics and an up-close-and-personal experience with the power of OUR CENTER-PIECE MILITARY jet fighter. You’ll see an amazing array of both civilian and military aircraft. Last year’s show drew over 40,000 people AND ENDED WITH A STELLAR PARTICIPATORY EVENT! with even more UNIQUE aircraft and performers, this year’s show will be even better! Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake is the ONLY Airshow held over WATER in the Southwest and is the 3rd largest air show in texas! benefiting charities:



The CCVF has been established to raise monies to assist with the physical and emotional recovery and rehabilitation of wounded, injured and disabled military personnel and veterans with a focus on those individuals residing in and organizations serving the Northeast Texas region. The Foundation’s principal fundraising activity is the Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake air show presented during the July 4th Holiday weekend each year. The CCVF is an all-volunteer, non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Your donation is tax-deductible as allowed by law..
