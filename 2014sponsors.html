Sponsorship Opportunities

You can build brand awareness and good will for your business by partnering with the Cedar Creek Veterans Foundation to sponsor the spectacular Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake Air Show. Sponsorships, beginning as low as $250, are available at varying levels to allow individuals and companies to show their support.

 

We can even tailor a package to meet your company’s goals. Sponsorships, beginning as low as $250, are available at varying levels to allow individuals and companies to show their support. We can even tailor a package to meet your company’s goals.


BENEFITS OF SPONSORSHIP INCLUDE:

Your company’s name and logo in front of the HUGE audience for the Air Show

Opportunity to help wounded veterans rebuild their lives.

Recognition on FM 95.9 KCKL during their onsite broadcast during the Air Show.

Premium Air Show seating at the Pinnacle Club for sponsors and their guests (Silver level and above) or priority access to purchase premium tickets.

Tickets to the Pilot’s Party and a chance to get photos and autographs (Platinum level and above), or priority access to purchase Pilot Party tickets.


Contact Gayle Robinson (903-451-5054) or Patty Evans (903-451-1020) to design a sponsor package that fits your needs.

benefiting charities:

 


The CCVF foundation has been established to raise monies to assist with the physical and emotional recovery and rehabilitation of wounded, injured and disabled military personnel and veterans with a focus on those individuals residing in and organizations serving the Northeast Texas region. The foundation’s principal fundraising activity is the Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake air show presented during the July 4th Holiday weekend each year. The CCVF is an all-volunteer, non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Your donation is tax-deductible as allowed by law
