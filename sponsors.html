Sponsorship Opportunities

You can build brand awareness and good will for your business by partnering with the Cedar Creek Veterans Foundation (CCVF) to sponsor the spectacular Thunder Over Cedar Creek Lake Air Show. Sponsorships, beginning as low as $250, are available at varying levels to allow individuals and companies to show their support.

 

We can even tailor a package to meet your company’s goals. Sponsorships, beginning as low as $250, are available at varying levels to allow individuals and companies to show their support for our military veterans and their families. We can even tailor a package to meet your company’s goals.


BENEFITS OF SPONSORSHIP INCLUDE:

Your company’s name and logo in front of the HUGE audience for the Air Show

Opportunity to help wounded veterans rebuild their lives.

Recognition on FM 95.9 KCKL during their onsite broadcast during the Air Show.

Premium Air Show seating at the Pinnacle Club for sponsors and their guests (Silver level and above) or priority access to purchase premium tickets.

Tickets to the Pilot’s Party and a chance to get photos and autographs (Platinum level and above), or priority access to purchase Pilot Party tickets.


Contact THE CCVF AT 903 - 451 - 3291 to design a sponsor package that fits your needs.
